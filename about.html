Hi, I’m Litzy. I’m a multimedia designer born and raised in San Diego, California. I recently graduated from SDSU with a bachelor’s degree in Multimedia and a minor in Digital and Social Media. I like creating things that feel intentional and personal, whether that’s through design, web, video, or more hands-on work. Right now, I’m interested in continuing to explore different interests, paying attention to what feels meaningful, and eventually creating something of my own.

Frequently Asked Questions

I chose multimedia because I’ve always been a bit of a jack of all trades. I knew I wanted to pursue something that would let me explore different paths and not feel limited to one thing. I like having that flexibility. I always think about the saying, “a jack of all trades is a master of none, but oftentimes better than a master of one.”

I find a lot of inspiration in femininity, collaboration, and social justice. I’m especially drawn to intersectional feminism and being part of work that supports people and communities. Even when it’s not super direct, like working with women or small businesses, I still feel connected to that. I really value community and being able to contribute to it in different ways.

I learned a lot about myself, especially when it comes to patience, kindness, and forgiveness. Once you’re able to give those things to yourself, it becomes a lot easier to extend them to others. I think people can feel that, and it creates stronger connections. That’s something I really value and want to carry with me moving forward.

Right now, I work as a personal and studio assistant for small businesses, and I want to keep learning from those experiences, especially how to build and optimize systems. I’m also interested in exploring teaching, particularly at the secondary level, and possibly continuing my education in art history at SDSU. I know a lot of this will take shape over time, and I’m excited to see where it leads.
